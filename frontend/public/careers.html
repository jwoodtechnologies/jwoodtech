Careers

Work on what matters.

We're a small, focused team building things that genuinely move the needle for our clients. Everyone here works on real problems with real consequences — not internal tools or speculative features that never ship.

We hire for craft, curiosity, and character. If you care deeply about the quality of your work and want to operate alongside people who do the same, you'll fit here.

What we value.

I

High Standards

We hold the bar high — for our work, our communication, and our commitments. Average is never acceptable here.

II

Ownership

You own what you build. That means the pride of great work and the accountability when something needs fixing.

III

Honest Communication

We say what we mean, directly and with respect. No politics, no performance — just clear thinking and honest feedback.

IV

Continuous Learning

The best people here are always getting better. We invest in growth and expect curiosity as a baseline, not a bonus.

Open Roles

No open positions. We're not actively hiring right now, but we're always interested in exceptional people.

If you believe your skills and values align with what we're building, we'd still love to hear from you. Reach out with what you do and how you think.
