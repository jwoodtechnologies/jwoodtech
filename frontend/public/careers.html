Careers

Work on what
matters.

We're a small, serious team building intelligent systems for businesses that demand the best. If that's the kind of work that gets you up in the morning, you may belong here.

We don't hire for headcount. When we bring someone on, it's because we believe they'll change the shape of what we're capable of.

What we look for
I
Ownership

You treat the work as yours. You don't wait to be told what needs to happen — you see it, you do it, you own the outcome.

II
Precision

Good enough isn't in your vocabulary. You care deeply about the details — because that's where the difference between mediocre and excellent lives.

III
Curiosity

You're genuinely excited by hard problems. You read, you experiment, you stay ahead of the curve — not because you have to, but because you want to.

IV
Directness

You say what you think, respectfully and clearly. You prefer honest feedback to comfortable silence.

Open Roles

We're heads-down building right now. When we're ready to bring someone on, you'll hear it here first.

—
No open positions.

We grow deliberately. Check back when the time is right — or introduce yourself now and be first in line.
