About the Founder

Founded by
Jacob J. Wood

JWood Tech was founded in 2020, emerging from the COVID-19 pandemic with a clear mission: help businesses not just survive the digital shift, but lead it. Headquartered in Utah, USA, we specialize in AI-powered application development, full-stack web and mobile platforms, e-commerce systems built for scale, and digital transformation consulting.

Over the years, we've built platforms and commerce systems for hundreds of companies across industries — from startups to established enterprises. Alongside our client work, we develop proprietary in-house technology that we believe will shape how businesses operate in the years ahead.

We sit at the intersection of business intelligence, artificial intelligence, and creative technology — building for other companies while quietly building our own future.

"We don't just build for the present — we build for what's next."
